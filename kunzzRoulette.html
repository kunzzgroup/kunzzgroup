Game Overview

Vegas Roulette is a classic casino game where you bet on where a ball will land on a spinning wheel. The wheel has 37 numbers (0-36), with 0 being green, and 1-36 split between red and black.

Starting Balance

You begin with $1,000. Make smart bets to grow your bankroll!

How to Play

Step 1: Select your chip value ($1, $5, $10, $25, $100, or $500)

Step 2: Click on betting spots to place your chips

Step 3: Click "SPIN WHEEL" to start the game

Step 4: Watch the wheel spin for 4 seconds

Step 5: Collect your winnings automatically!

Betting Options & Payouts

Bet Type Description Payout
Single Number Any number 0-36 35:1
Red/Black 18 red or 18 black numbers 1:1
Even/Odd All even or odd numbers (0 excluded) 1:1
1-18 / 19-36 Low or high half 1:1
Dozens 1-12, 13-24, or 25-36 2:1
Columns One of three columns 2:1
Green (0) Zero only 35:1

Number Colors

Red Numbers: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 12, 14, 16, 18, 19, 21, 23, 25, 27, 30, 32, 34, 36

Black Numbers: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 11, 13, 15, 17, 20, 22, 24, 26, 28, 29, 31, 33, 35

Green Number: 0

Tips for Success

  • Start with small bets to learn the game
  • Mix inside bets (numbers) with outside bets (colors, ranges)
  • Watch the history to spot patterns (or just for fun!)
  • Set a budget and stick to it
  • Remember: The house always has an edge!

Game Controls

SPIN WHEEL: Start the spin (requires at least one bet)

CLEAR BETS: Remove all bets and refund chips

Game Over

The game ends when your balance reaches $0. Refresh the page to play again!
